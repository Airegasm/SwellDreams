# 🎈 SwellDreams v6.1 — The Big Overhaul is Live

This is a **massive** release — pretty much every core system got rebuilt or replaced. Update and dig in:

## ✨ What's new

🎭 **Unified Dynamic Character Cards** — single, group, and instructor cards are now ONE card type. Build whatever you want from one editor. Ships with a **Lana & Scarlett hybrid example** so you can see a full group card in action.

👥 **On-the-fly Multi-Character** — turn any card into a group (or back) just by adding/removing members on the base card. Per-member descriptions, attributes, voices, and even per-member targeting for custom buttons.

🧑‍🏫 **Instructor Mode** — a leaner, "straight to business" inflation partner/guide. Less roleplay fluff, more direct hands-on direction.

📝 **Dynamic Author's Note** — now lives per-card instead of one global blob, so each character can steer the AI its own way.

🎯 **Redesigned Checkpoint System** — far more control over how scenes escalate; steer the story and trigger events by capacity.

💾 **Persistent Chat Sessions** — every character remembers its last conversation. Jump between characters without losing anything; hit **New** when you want a clean slate. Crash/close safe.

🔌 **Automatic Pump Catalog + Manual Pump Mode** — calibration now lives with the *pump*, not the outlet, with per-pump limits. Plus manual bulb/bike pump support.

🎲 **Minigames Editor** — build interactive games right into your scenarios.

⚡ **Triggers replace Flows** — the old Flow system is deprecated in favor of a new, more powerful Trigger system.

📚 **Real Lorebook Support** — proper Dictionary/Library system for world info and character lore (with V2/V3 import).

🧠 **Restructured Main Prompt** — cleaner, more reliable AI behavior under the hood.

⚙️ **Settings Overhaul** — reorganized, decluttered, and a lot easier to navigate.

🆓 **AI Horde Support** — free cloud LLM access built in, so you can play even without your own model or hardware.

…and a ton of smaller polish throughout.

## ⚠️ Heads up

This was a huge rewrite, so things might be a little weird or buggy in spots. If you hit anything strange, **please let me know** — bug reports help a lot. Have fun! 🎈
